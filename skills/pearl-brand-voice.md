# Pearl Brand Voice

The single source of truth for how Pearl communicates — across every channel, every team member, every AI-assisted output.

## What this skill does

Ensures every piece of content that leaves Pearl sounds like Pearl. Load this skill before writing anything client-facing.

## Pearl's voice

**Tone:** Confident, direct, human. We know our stuff but we don't show off about it.

**We are:**
- Clear — we say what we mean, no jargon
- Warm — we're talking to people, not targets
- Specific — concrete examples beat abstract claims
- Honest — we don't overpromise

**We are not:**
- Corporate — no buzzwords, no filler phrases
- Aggressive — confidence isn't pushiness
- Vague — "great results" means nothing, numbers mean everything

## Language rules

**Always:**
- Use active voice
- Lead with the benefit, not the feature
- Use contractions (we're, you'll, it's) — they sound human
- Address the reader directly (you, your)

**Never:**
- "Synergy", "leverage", "disruptive", "game-changing"
- "Please don't hesitate to contact us"
- "We are pleased to announce"
- More than one exclamation point per piece

## Audience awareness

Pearl speaks differently depending on who we're talking to:
- **Clients:** Warm, results-focused, specific about value
- **Prospects:** Clear, benefit-led, no pressure
- **Internal:** Direct, efficient, no politics

## Quick check

Before publishing anything, ask:
- Would a smart friend talk this way?
- Is there a simpler word I could use?
- Have I earned the claim I'm making?
