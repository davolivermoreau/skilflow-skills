# Professional Email Tone

Transform any draft email into clear, professional communication that sounds human — not corporate.

## What this skill does

Takes a rough email draft and rewrites it to be:
- Clear and direct — says what it means without padding
- Professional without being cold
- Appropriately concise — respects the reader's time
- Action-oriented — makes the next step obvious

## How to use

Paste your draft email and say "rewrite this using my email tone skill."

## Tone principles

**DO:**
- Lead with the most important point
- Use short sentences and paragraphs
- Name the specific action you need
- Set a clear timeline when relevant

**AVOID:**
- "Hope this email finds you well"
- "Please don't hesitate to reach out"
- "As per my previous email"
- Passive voice when active is clearer
- More than one ask per email

## Output format

Rewrite the email maintaining the sender's intent. If the subject line is weak, suggest a better one. Flag if the ask is unclear before rewriting.

## Example

**Input:** "Hi, just wanted to follow up on the thing we discussed, let me know if you need anything, thanks"

**Output:**
Subject: Following up on [topic] — next steps?

Hi [Name],

Following up on [specific topic] from [when].

Can you confirm [specific ask] by [date]?

Happy to jump on a quick call if easier.

[Name]
